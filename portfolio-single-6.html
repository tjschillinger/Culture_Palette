A Photography Site Clients Want to Use

Marina, a mother of two toddlers and a mortgage, came to us because she needed a web presence for her photography business. She was unsatisfied with word-of-mouth marketing that was giving a new client every few months. While on a budget, her site needed to feature a gallery, an about section, package information, and a way to contact her.

We first defined where all the features could live on her site. Working with her, we decided to make her site 3 pages, with the landing page pulling most of the weight. It told a story of what she did, who she was, and how to contact her. The rest of the site was devoted to different packages and their details.

We presented the developed product, and Marina was thrilled with the results. She tells us that people contact her weekly because of the site.
